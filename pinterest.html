Pinterest strategy for VisionText AI

Profile Setup

Profile Name: TZNtech

Bio: TZNtech shares VisionText AI ideas, image-to-text tips, and simple content for e-commerce teams, resellers, and busy small businesses.

Target Audience: Small store owners, marketplace sellers, social commerce teams, student entrepreneurs, and admin staff who manage image-based text.

Content Strategy

  • Post simple educational pins that solve real seller problems.
  • Use before-and-after examples to show clear value.
  • Mix static pins, carousel pins, and idea pin formats.
  • Link pins to the product, pricing, and contact pages.
  • Keep titles short, useful, and keyword-friendly.
