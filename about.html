About The Project

VisionText AI is a classroom website concept by TZNtech

This project presents a simple business idea from TZNtech, with clear product pages and a marketing plan using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Project Goal

The goal is to show how TZNtech can present an e-commerce style digital product clearly. It also demonstrates responsive design, page structure, and simple front-end interaction.

What Is Real?

The website design, layout, and content are real. The AI upload system and dashboard are demo sections only. They do not connect to a backend or database.

Core Message

Why this idea is useful

Problem

Important business text is often locked inside images and screenshots.

Solution

VisionText AI converts those images into editable and reusable text.

Value

The concept saves time, supports organization, and helps small teams work faster under the TZNtech brand.
